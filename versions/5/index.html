Integrate Business Entities from Informatica in Data360

Realize the full potential of the curated and enriched business entities from Informatica directly in D360. In this step by step guide, we will work though the steps required to operationalize business entities created in Informatica in D360

Step Summary

1. Connect to Informatica System
2. Choose Business Entity
3. Review and Extend Mappings
4. Validate Connected data
5. Set up Identity Rules
6. Validate Identity Data
7. Enable sync to Informatica
Connect to Informatica System

Detailed instructions for connecting to the Informatica system will appear here. This step involves configuring the connection parameters and establishing secure communication channels.

Choose Business Entity

Select the business entity you want to integrate from Informatica. Review the available entities and choose the one that matches your requirements.

Review and Extend Mappings

Review the default field mappings between Informatica and Data360. Extend or modify mappings as needed to ensure data integrity and completeness.

Validate Connected data

Validate the connected data to ensure accuracy and completeness. Run validation checks and review any discrepancies before proceeding.

Set up Identity Rules

Configure identity rules to determine how records are matched and merged. Set up matching criteria and rules for duplicate detection.

Validate Identity Data

Validate the identity data to ensure proper matching and merging. Review identity resolution results and adjust rules if necessary.

Enable sync to Informatica

Enable synchronization to Informatica to keep data in sync. Configure sync schedules and options for bidirectional data flow.

Setup Experiences

  • Search Before Create - prevent duplicate records from being created
  • Copy field - enrich operational records with enterprise attributes
  • Related List - showcase any information related to the primary entity
